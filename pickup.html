Duration

Role

Interaction Design | Research | Prototyping

Project Overview

Studies have shown that even modest amounts of exercise can help people feel more energetic throughout the day, sleep better at night, have sharper memories, and gain a more positive outlook on themselves. Given how stressful and competitive Cornell could be, we wanted to design a product that helped students stay active and feel connected with each other through shared interests in group sports. Our team is passionate about creating a community behind physical, emotional, and mental wellness, as this is something the Cornell campus could truly benefit from.
